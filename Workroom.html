Meetings that feel

Ditch the grid of floating heads. In Workroom, you inhabit a real space — and conversations find you naturally.

2D Office Explorer

Move your avatar with WASD or arrow keys. Walk into meeting rooms, drop into the lounge, or sneak away to focus zones — all rendered in a beautiful top-down view.

Persistent Chat & Reactions

Rooms have their own chat threads. React to messages, start polls, share files, and set room topics — all right inside your office without switching apps.

Choose your look from hundreds of skins, accessories, and cosmetics — many earned through gameplay. Your avatar becomes a unique expression of you in the digital space.

Lock rooms to specific team members. Set guest access, whiteboard-only zones, or admin-only spaces. Enterprise-grade access control with zero complexity.
